Voters Approval and sentiments towards candidate

After analyzing the last 20,000 tweets for the month of December on both Buhari and Atiku twitster handle, this are some of the insights we gather.

-- Voters ratings for candidate Buhari has climbed to 52% while Atiku has also climbed to 51% it's kind of neck and neck.

--Some of the key words associated wih Buhari when voters mentioned his names are "bvn ,tsa, which indicate the government policy on improving corruption is still very popular , there is an uproar on Gov Umuahi of Ebonyi joining APC, also there has been 2 attacks by Boko-Haram, 1 in Zamfara and revelation by Galadima against Buhari's family, despite this the president has increased in popularity.

--Some of the key words associated with Atiku when voters mentioned his names are "loss ,deeply, Saddened". Which is in my opinion indicate the gesture of Atiku reaching out to the wife/ family of the slain soldier by boko-haram, which in my opinion is a positive move, while on the negative side voters still believe that the delegates were bought for Atiku to win the primaries.

Voters Approval and sentiments towards Parties

After analyzing the last 20,000 tweets on both APC and PDP twitter handle in the month of December, this are some of the insights we gather.

-- Voters ratings for APC is 51% down 22% from last month while PDP climbed 6% to 50%.

-- The party has taken a hit in voters sentiment, It has come under immense attack from opposition as the election campaign become intense. There are also attacks from boko-haram and revelation from Galadima about Buhari's family owning billions in assets, when Buhari was in office, this has really damaged the APC brand, even though it did not affect the president rating the party took the knock.

-- On the other side the PDP is riding on inflicting damages on the ruling party as voters sentiment increase towards them with 6% increase from last month.

Opinion and Verdicts

Voters with No employment are more likely to vote Sowore compare to other candidate apart from Buhari and Atiku, voters who employed are more likely to vote Ezekwezili compare to other candidate apart from the 2 major candidates Female voters are more likely to vote for OTHERS compared to the rest apart from the 2 major candidates, male voters are more likely to vote for Ezekwezili. Sowore is popular amongst voters with no income and low income, while ezekwezili is popular among higher income earners if both Buhari and Atiku are excluded from the analysis.

On voters sentiments Buhari and Atiku has similar approval ratings. But APC approval has taken a knock since the campaign started the ruling party had to contend with series of negative attacks from opposition party and also attacks from boko-haram, there has been a 6% increase in voters sentiments towards PDP. Despite this Atiku has slipped in the poll by 5.3%,the decrease is driving largely by adding 2 more candidates to the poll, this 2 candidates took voting share off Atiku while Buhari increase his polling share by 2.2%. This is the strange thing about Nigeria elections, in other climes you would expect the Buhari to be trailing in the polls given all the negativity , but alas this is not the case. Buhari is leading by wide margin in North-West and North-East, he is leading by a lower margin in North-Central and South-West, while Atiku is leading by wide margin in South-East and a lower margin in South-South.Buhari on 59.2% , Atiku 34.7%. The verdict is Buhari to win as of today.

Polls Assumption

  • The sampling is based on 1000 participants between 23 November- 23 December 2018
  • The poll is based on social media participants, with voters who have PVC.
  • The sampling is based on exact turn out and voting weights per region in last elections
  • The margin of error is +-3% , meaning the result could be between +3% or -3% of actual results of polls.
  • Recent events might not be captured in this analysis but will be captured in the next polling results in December 2018
  • The ratio of male to female in this poll is 10:1
